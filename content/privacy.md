This policy explains what data is collected on **The Korea Wave** (this website) and how third-party advertising vendors may use cookies here.

## Advertising cookies

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visits to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google's Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/). Alternatively, users can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting [www.aboutads.info](https://www.aboutads.info/choices/).

## Analytics cookies

If we enable an analytics service (for example, Google Analytics) on this site, that service may set its own cookies to measure how the site is used in aggregate. We do not currently combine analytics data with any personal account information, because this site does not have user accounts.

## What we do not collect

We do not require registration or accounts to read this site, and we do not knowingly collect personal information beyond what your browser and any advertising or analytics vendor cookies provide, as described above.

## Visitors in the EU/EEA and UK

If you access this site from the European Union, European Economic Area, or United Kingdom, the cookie-based advertising and analytics described above may qualify as processing of personal data under the GDPR/UK GDPR. You can withdraw consent to personalized advertising at any time via the opt-out links above, and you can block cookies generally through your browser settings.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our advertising or analytics setup changes. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
