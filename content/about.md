**The Korea Wave** covers Korean entertainment — K-pop, K-drama, film, and webtoons — as a global business, for readers who follow the industry and strategy behind Korean culture's international growth, not just its releases.

## What we cover

We analyze agency performance and strategy, streaming and fandom-platform economics, content export deals, and IP licensing — the business decisions that shape how Korean entertainment reaches the world.

## How our articles are made

Each article synthesizes information from multiple original Korean-language sources — company disclosures, trade press, and industry data — into a single analytical piece. We do not translate or republish any single source article; every piece is an original synthesis written from several sources at once.

We use AI-assisted drafting tools as part of our production process. **Every article is reviewed and approved by a human editor before publication.** No article is published automatically or without human sign-off. As our editorial process matures, some review steps may become more automated, but human oversight of what gets published remains a fixed part of how we operate.
